About Me

I am a passionate Trainer

Hi there, my name is Akash Shetty, and I am a passionate full stack web development trainer. With years of experience in the field, I specialize in teaching front-end technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, as well as back-end technologies like Node.js, MongoDB, and Express.
